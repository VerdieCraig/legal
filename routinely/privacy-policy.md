# Privacy Policy for Routinely

**Effective Date:** April 15, 2026  
**Last Updated:** April 15, 2026

## Overview

Routinely ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information Collection and Use

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.**

### Data Storage

All data created within the App, including:
- Your routine checklists and items
- Daily progress and completion history
- App settings and preferences

...is stored **locally on your device only** using your device's local storage. This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not transmitted to any servers
- Is not shared with any third parties
- Is not accessible to the developer

### No Account Required

The App does not require you to create an account, provide an email address, or share any personal information.

### No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use:
- Analytics services
- Tracking technologies
- Cookies
- Advertising identifiers
- Third-party data collection tools

## In-App Purchases

Routinely offers an optional one-time in-app purchase ("Unlock Full Version") processed through Google Play Billing. We do not collect or store your payment information. All purchase transactions are handled entirely by Google Play and are subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We only receive confirmation of whether a purchase was completed — no payment details, card numbers, or financial information are shared with us.

## Third-Party Services

The App integrates with Google Play Billing solely for the purpose of processing the optional in-app purchase. No user data is shared with Google Play beyond what is necessary to complete the transaction. We do not integrate with any other third-party services.

## Data Security

Your data remains on your device and is protected by your device's built-in security measures. If you uninstall the App, all locally stored data will be permanently deleted from your device.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The App is suitable for all ages and does not require any personal information from users of any age.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

## Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:
- You can delete individual items within a routine using the delete function
- You can delete entire routines from the Settings screen
- Uninstalling the App will permanently delete all data

## Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data for us to access, modify, or delete on your behalf. You have complete control over your data through the App's interface and your device settings.
