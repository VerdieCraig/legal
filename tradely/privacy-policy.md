# Privacy Policy for Tradely

**Effective Date:** April 21, 2026
**Last Updated:** April 21, 2026

## Overview

Tradely ("the App") is developed by Dark Lotus Dev. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Data You Provide

When you use Tradely, you create business records including:

- Client names and contact information
- Invoice and estimate details
- Line items, pricing, and notes
- Business name, address, and logo (entered in Settings)

For **free tier users**, this data is stored locally on your device only.

For **Pro subscribers**, this data is additionally stored in Google Firebase Firestore (cloud storage) to enable sync across devices. This data is associated with your Google account and retained on our servers for as long as your subscription is active, plus 30 days following cancellation.

### Automatically Collected Data

**Crash Reports (all users):** We use Google Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports and diagnostic information when the App encounters an error. This may include device model, OS version, and a stack trace of the crash. No personally identifiable information from your business records is included in crash reports.

**Usage Analytics (all users):** We use Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymized usage data, including events such as invoice created, PDF shared, subscription started, and sync completed. This data helps us understand how the App is used and improve it. It does not include the content of your business records.

### Purchase Information

Subscriptions are processed through **RevenueCat** and **Google Play Billing**. We receive confirmation of your subscription status and entitlement — we do not receive or store your payment method, card numbers, or financial information. All payment processing is handled by Google Play and governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

## How We Use Your Information

- To provide and operate the App's features
- To sync your business records across devices (Pro subscribers only)
- To diagnose crashes and improve app stability
- To understand feature usage and guide product development
- To manage your subscription status and unlock Pro features

## Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with the following service providers, solely to operate the App:

| Service | Purpose | Data Involved |
|---|---|---|
| Google Firebase Firestore | Cloud sync | Your business records (Pro only) |
| Google Firebase Crashlytics | Crash reporting | Device info, crash traces |
| Google Firebase Analytics | Usage analytics | Anonymized usage events |
| RevenueCat | Subscription management | Subscription status |
| Google Play Billing | Payment processing | Handled entirely by Google |

Each of these services operates under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review Google's Privacy Policy and RevenueCat's Privacy Policy for details.

## Data Retention

**Free tier users:** All data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the App removes all local data. Android Auto Backup may preserve a copy per Google's backup policies.

**Pro subscribers:** Your business records are retained in Firestore for the duration of your active subscription plus 30 days following cancellation. If you resubscribe within 30 days, your data is restored. After 30 days, cloud data is deleted and cannot be recovered.

## Data Security

Pro subscriber data is stored in Google Firebase Firestore, which uses industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. Access is restricted to your account. We do not have direct access to your individual business records.

## Children's Privacy

The App is intended for use by adults conducting business. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has used the App in a way that provided us their information, please contact us.

## Your Rights and Choices

- You may export all your data at any time using the **Export Data** feature in Settings (produces a JSON file you can save or share)
- You may delete individual records within the App at any time
- Pro subscribers may request deletion of their Firestore data by contacting us — we will process deletion within 30 days
- You may opt out of Firebase Analytics by adjusting your device's advertising settings

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document. For material changes, we will make reasonable effort to notify users within the App. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
