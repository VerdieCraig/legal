## 2. Description of Service

Tradely is a mobile application designed to help independent tradespeople manage clients, create estimates and invoices, generate professional PDFs, and maintain their business records. The App is available in two tiers:

**Free Tier:** Includes up to 3 clients and 5 total invoices/estimates, local storage only, and manual data export. Android Auto Backup is enabled as a baseline data protection measure.

**Pro Tier:** Includes unlimited clients, invoices, and estimates; business logo support; and cloud sync across devices via secure cloud storage. Pro is available as a monthly or annual subscription.
